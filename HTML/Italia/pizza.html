Pizza

Contents:

  • 175 g Olymel bologna, sliced or diced
  • 2 naan-style flatbreads
  • 100 ml store-bought pizza sauce
  • 175 g mixed grated pizza cheese
  • 8 white mushrooms, finely sliced
  • ¼ green pepper, cut into strips
  • Italian herbs (optional)

Instruction:

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F. (190° C.).
  2. Place flatbreads on pizza pan. Top with sauce, sliced mushrooms, bologna and cheese, ending with green pepper strips. Sprinkle with Italian herbs.
  3. Bake until cheese is golden, about 15 minutes.
